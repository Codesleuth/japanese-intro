Why Learn Japanese?

  • World's third largest economy
  • Culture baked into the language
  • Flows, makes sense, sounds great!

Why not Learn Japanese?

  • It's very different from English
  • Three character sets - including Chinese
  • Offending people is easy
  • Easy to forget if not used

A Brief History

Chinese characters were adopted 6th to 9th century, called "kanji"

By the 12th century, "hiragana" and "katakana" had been created from kanji

This gave the language the freedom it needed to grow

Since the mid 18th century, "gairaigo" (foreign borrowed words) have been used

Mainly English

Also from Portuguese, Dutch and Spanish

Almost always written in katakana

A Conversation!

  1. Introduce yourselfes to eachother
  2. Ask eachother
    "how are you"?
  3. Ask eachother
    "where are you from"?
  4. Tell eachother
    "I can speak English, not Japanese"?

Do

  • Remember someone's name!
  • Try to be polite
    (use "please" & "thank you")
  • Carefully inspect a business card,
    and place it down neatly in front of you
  • Try and learn phrases!

Don't

  • Forget someone's name!
  • Say "you" unless you really have to!
