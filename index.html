Why Learn Japanese?

Why not Learn Japanese?

  • Three character sets - including Chinese
  • Offending people is easy
  • Easy to forget if not used

A Brief History

Chinese characters were adopted between the sixth to ninth century, called kanji

By the 12th century, hiragana and katakana had been created from kanji

This gave the language the freedom it needed to grow

Since the mid 18th century, "gairaigo" (foreign borrowed words) have been popular in Japan, mainly taken from English

There are also words from Portuguese, Dutch and Spanish, thought to have originated from overseas merchants

Examples:

  • テブル / teburu / table
  • ビリ / biru / beer
  • タクシ / takushi / taxi
  • ホテル / hoteru / hotel
